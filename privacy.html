Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you. Period.

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Overview

LiveFilm is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe that your photos, your data, and your creative work belong exclusively to you. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information — or more accurately, how we don't.

Information We Don't Collect

Your Photos

All photos and Live Photos captured with LiveFilm are stored locally on your device. We never upload, access, or process your images on any server.

Personal Information

We don't collect your name, email, location, or any other personal identifiers. No account creation is required to use our app.

Usage Data

We don't track how you use the app, which features you access, or how often you shoot. Your creative process is yours alone.

Device Information

We don't collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or technical specifications. Your device information stays on your device.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data created by LiveFilm is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Photos & Live Photos: Saved to your device's Photos library, entirely under your control.
  • App Settings: Saved using iOS standard UserDefaults, stored locally on your device.
  • Custom LUTs: Imported .cube files remain on your device and are never uploaded.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties. Our app does not integrate with:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks or tracking pixels
  • Social media SDKs or data collection frameworks
  • Cloud storage services (unless you explicitly choose to back up via iCloud)

App Store and Apple

When you download our app from the App Store, Apple may collect certain information as part of their standard operations, including:

  • App download and purchase information
  • Crash logs and technical diagnostics (if you opt in)
  • App Store usage analytics

This data collection is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, not ours. We do not have access to this information.

Device Permissions

Our app may request access to the following device features:

Camera
Required to capture Live Photos with film filters. Photos are stored locally.
Photos Library
Required to save captured Live Photos and import LUT files. Access is limited to your explicit actions.

Data Security

While we don't store your data on our servers, we take the following measures to protect your local data:

  • App data is protected by iOS standard security mechanisms
  • Photos exported to your Photos library inherit your device's security settings
  • No network transmission of your photos or data occurs within our app

Your Rights

Because all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it:

  • Access: All your photos are immediately accessible within the Photos library.
  • Deletion: You can delete any photo or app data at any time.
  • Export: You can share your Live Photos as you see fit.
  • App Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all associated local data from your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect personal information from anyone, our app is inherently compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA and GDPR-K.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@latentmanifest.app
